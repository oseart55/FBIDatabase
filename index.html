What is this?

This is just a project I created to make looking at FBI Wanted and Seeking Information. There really isn't a difference in using this page, or the FBI Office page Here all of the information is derived from the Offical Page, and any questions or comments about the information should be directed to the appropriate Field Office which you can find Here. Any issue regarding the site itself, functionality, or useage (even recommended functionality or changes) can be directed to me and you can contact me here under "Contact".

How do I use this page?

When the page is loaded, a number of Persons will populate onto the page. Each entry will have an Image, Name, Subject, More Info Button, and Last Modified Date. The Subject will be related to why they are on the list, for example: "Seeking Information" will pertain to an event in which the FBI is looking to obtain information. "Violent Crime" relates to... well.. Violent Crime... ect.

Using the Search box at the top allows you to search for a particular name, and if there are any results they will be returned on the page. Selecting the "Search" button, or pressing the Enter Key will initiate the Search. Selecting the "Clear" button will clear the Search, and reload the first results from the FBI Database.

Selecting the "More Info" button will open a modal that has more information regarding the entry. At the bottom of each will be Field Office Information for the Field Office that is in charge of the entry (I think).
